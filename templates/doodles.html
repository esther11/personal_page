Doodles rather than paintings

After 13 years, I decided to pick up my hobby in childhood - doodling. Check out the doodles I made at 1994 and 2016, I did make some progress :)
Well, regardless of creativity, in terms of which my capacity might have deteriorated...

Doodling is fun. I would not call this recreation painting, since paintings are art, touching masterpieces, stories behind the papers or canvases. Paintings are charming, but the process of making one? It takes too much that I would rather choose to doodle.

However, I shall salute all great painters. There are not many of them today.
